## Set up your account

Europe only

### Register to sell across Europe

Seller Central registration is available in English, Japanese, Chinese, and European languages. Once you have registered, you can manage your Seller Central account in your preferred language. You will need the following to register to sell in the EU:

1. Your business information including name, address, phone number, and email address
2. An internationally-chargeable credit card
3. Bank account information: A valid bank account is needed to receive payment for goods sold
4. An address in Europe for customer returns
