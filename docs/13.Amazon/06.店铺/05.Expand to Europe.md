Expand to Europe

United Kingdom → Register

Answer a few questions to get started

1. Where is your business registered?
   Business location: United States
2. What type of business do you have?
   Business type: Privately-owned business

As a Privately-owned business from United States, you will need:

- Valid government-issued photo ID or passport
- Recent proof of address (e.g. utility bill, credit card statement) -- issued within 180 days
- Recent bank statement (within 180 days) -- account holder name must match your name or business name
- Business registration document (e.g. Articles of Incorporation/Organization/Formation, Certificate of Good Standing)
- Letter of Authorisation -- only required if you are not the business owner
- Chargeable credit or debit card
- Mobile phone number capable of receiving SMS
- Additional documents may be requested by email if needed
