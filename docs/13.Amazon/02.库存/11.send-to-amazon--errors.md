Effective March 31, 2026, only sellers with the Brand Representative selling role in Amazon Brand Registry will be eligible to use manufacturer barcodes (for example, UPC, ISBN). [Learn more](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G200141480)

## 4 Errors

- This product is ineligible for sending to Amazon with a manufacturer barcode. Please remove the product from the shipment, re-create the offer with an Amazon barcode, and label your units. [Learn more](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G9P8W8RZ96JFHVZH)
- Product details missing essential information
- This product is missing necessary information. Please edit the listing and enter a title.
- This product does not exist in the destination marketplace catalog. The necessary product information will need to be provided before it can be inbounded.

## Available selling roles

There are two selling roles that an Administrator can assign:

- Brand Representative: A selling partner who is directly employed by your brand
- Reseller: An external, third-party selling partner who sells your brand’s products on Amazon
